The Creation Narrative

A BibleProject Translation

Genesis 1:1-2:3

Title

1In the beginning, Elohim created the skies and the land.
בְּרֵאשִׁ֖ית בָּרָ֣א אֱלֹהִ֑ים אֵ֥ת הַשָּׁמַ֖יִם וְאֵ֥ת הָאָֽרֶץ

Pre-Creation State

2Now, the land was wild and waste
וְהָאָ֗רֶץ הָיְתָ֥ה תֹ֙הוּ֙ וָבֹ֔הוּ
and darkness was over the face of the watery deep,
וְחֹ֖שֶׁךְ עַל פְּנֵ֥י תְה֑וֹם
but the breath/wind of Elohim was hovering over the face of the waters.
וְר֣וּחַ אֱלֹהִ֔ים מְרַחֶ֖פֶת עַל פְּנֵ֥י הַמָּֽיִם

Day 7: Completion & Rest

1And they were completed, the skies and the land and all their hosts,
וַיְכֻלּ֛וּ הַשָּׁמַ֥יִם וְהָאָ֖רֶץ וְכָל צְבָאָֽם

3And Elohim blessed the seventh day and he set it apart as holy,

because on it he rested from all his work which Elohim created to make.
כִּ֣י ב֤וֹ שָׁבַת֙ מִכָּל מְלַאכְתּ֖וֹ אֲשֶׁר בָּרָ֥א אֱלֹהִ֖ים לַֽעֲשׂוֹת
